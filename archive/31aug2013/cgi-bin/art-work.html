Art Ideas for sale

 

Once an idea has been sold and described in full detail, the idea is passed on with a contract of confidentiality where artist Matthew Roy Arnold passes all intellectual property over and will deny all aspect of the transaction taken place.

 

 

Self Portraites

 

The paintings were produced by individual painters asked by Matthew to paint a self portrait and not to sign the work. The only restrictions specified were of size and medium. After the work is painted, Matthew signs the work claiming the piece as his own work. When a painting is sold it gets replaced by a new painting by a different painter.

 

Discs

 

Discs are series of oil painted glass discs. Each glass disc is mounted by a mirror screw on the back and can be individually spun on and off a screw to be replaced with a different disc. The work can be screwed individually into a wall or for smaller pieces can be mounted onto a board witch is then hung.

 

The Discs are available through commission only.

Interaction

 

Interaction is a series of paintings in which the emphasis is on the interaction of two unfamiliar objects. These works could not be made without knowledge of the way in which the subjects can be manipulated. This series emphasizes the importance of an artists need to study there subject matter in certain situations.

Lost and Stole

 

Lost and Stolen works are commission based oil paintings where the patron lists significant things he or she has lost or had stolen. These are then painted onto a scene that suits the patron personality and life. The work will be updated whenever necessary.

The patron also owns all viewing and reproduction rights of the work.The work is only viewed by the artist Matthew during its production.
