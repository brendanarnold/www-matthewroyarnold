Individuals

Looking for an artwork to match your home? In the Taste Studies, individual spins are presented as ready-to-hang works which are uniquely matched to owner's tastes. Typically these works are on a framed glass background with a spin composition overlaid resulting in an attractive minimalist piece. Pre-made works from the Taste Studies are available occasionally put up for auction with no-reserve. To find out when this happens, subscribe to the news page on Facebook. Alternatively a more precise composition and palette can be specified to exactly suit your own requirements for as little as £30 – email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com for more details.

Looking for a unique gift? The Image Reaction Studies are tailored for individuals based on their interests. For example, the `Early Crimson' works shown were commissioned by an individual who supplied a series of giclée printed images of aviation and academic environments upon which the spins were overlaid. Prices begin at £40 with framed ready to hang versions for an extra £30 – again email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com for more details.

Corporate

Looking for a dynamic work that changes with season? The Place Reaction Studies can be installed in a composition and range of colours suitable for your own business environment. Alternate sets of discs can be supplied with easily swapped velcro mounts to allow the disc paintings to be changed throughout the year giving a dynamic, seasonal change of colours to the space. Prices start at £2,000 for a 3×5 m installation – for more details email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com
