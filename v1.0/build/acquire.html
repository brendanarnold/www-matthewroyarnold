ACQUIRING WORKS

INDIVIDUALS

In the Taste Studies, individual spins are presented as ready-to-hang works which are uniquely matched to owner's tastes. Typically these works are on a framed glass background with a spin composition overlaid resulting in an attractive minimalist piece. Pre-made works from the Taste Studies are occasionally put up for auction with no-reserve. To find out when this happens, subscribe to the news page on Facebook. Alternatively a more precise composition and palette can be specified to exactly suit your own requirements for as little as £30 – email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com for more details.

The Image Reaction Studies too can be tailored for individuals. The Early Crimson works were commissioned by an individual who supplied a series of digital images of aviation and academic environments upon which the spins were overlaid.
