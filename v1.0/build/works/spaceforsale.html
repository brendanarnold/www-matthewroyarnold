Art Space for Sale

About

Art Space for Sale attempts to quantify the commercial value of art. Comprised of a series of promotional paintings, drawings, scultures and t-shirts which were comissioned by various enterprises including Cadbury, DeWalt, Bristol Beer Factory, Bristol Fine Art, Magna and more in exchange for goods and services.

For more details, see the write-up of the Art Space for Sale event.
