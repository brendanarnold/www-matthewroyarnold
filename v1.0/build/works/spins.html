Spin Studies

About

The spins began in 2011 as abstract circular oil paintings on acrylic and glass discs and since then have developed through a series of studies involving a variety of media and contexts. Their abstract yet painterly nature serves as an ideal petri dish for wide ranging examinations into the perception and effects of art.

  • Image Reaction Studies
  • Place Reaction Studies
  • Taste Studies

For information on obtaining a spin study piece, see acquiring works.

Image Reaction Studies

The studies examine how overlaying abstract geometric spins over pre-existing images can imbue the spins with meaning and even purpose, thereby affecting the mood of the underlying image.

Place Reaction Studies

Similar to the Image Reaction Studies, Place Reaction Studies explores how a space can be editorialised by simple abstract spins on glass discs.

Taste Studies

The spins are presented in their most abstract form to determine preference in colour palette and composition. Pieces are judged based on sales on popular auction sites such as eBay.
