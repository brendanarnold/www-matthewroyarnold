WORKS

Spin Studies

The spin studies comprise of the Image Reaction Studies, the Place Reaction Studies and the Taste Studies each of which expand on the leitmotif of a simple geometric spin pattern.

Art Space for Sale

A write up of the Art Space for Sale event which explored the edges of commercial integrety in art.

Outsourced Self Portraits

Many artists no longer fabricate their own works, with the emphasis in these cases ostensibly being on the concept. These works explore how the nature of the work may be affected by outsourcing practices.

Art Ideas for Sale

Modern art practice is called into question when we consider through these works if concept too can be outsourced

Lost and Stolen

A continuously updated document of the things that have gone missing throughout a particular persons life.

Interaction Studies

Studies into using art to explore scenarios that other mediums cannot capture, in particular the impossible meeting of incongruous subjects.

Misc Works

Other individual works my Matthew Roy Arnold including the 'Sistine Table' and 'PCS10-The Good Shepherd'.
