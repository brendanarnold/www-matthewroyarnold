Outsourced Self Portraits

Many artists no longer fabricate their own works, with the emphasis in these cases ostensibly being on the concept. Exhibited in the Work show, these works explore how the nature of the work may be affected by outsourcing practices.

Art Ideas for Sale

An ongoing project where modern art practice is called into question when we consider through these works if concept too can be outsourced. Above linked are current ideas on offer. The Art Ideas for Sale were exhibited in the Work show.
