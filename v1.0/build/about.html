Biography

Artist in studio

Matthew was born and raised in Cleethorpes, UK and moved to Bristol in 2003 to study Fine Art at U.W.E. After graduating he worked in television as a prop maker to support his practice before becoming a full-time artist in 2011.

Themes

Much of Matthew's work looks at the nature and purpose of art and the artist's role within society.
