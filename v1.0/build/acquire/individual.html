Collecting original art is a rewarding activity and not as expensive as you might think, many of the works here are priced comparable to what you would spend in Ikea – plus you would be supporting a working artist.

Many of the Apparition Studies are available on the Etsy shop but also feel free to send an email to matthewroyarnold@gmail.com if you like something that you have seen on this site or would like to commission a piece.

Framed or unframed

An example framed Image Reaction Study

The Image Reaction Studies can be supplied with or without a frame. Framed pieces are ready to hang and are sandwiched between two panes of glass which hang about an inch away from the wall. The cost for the frame (and extra delivery) varies by size but is generally between an extra £5 and £35. Unframed works can be hung as you would a poster.

The Taste Studies all come pre-framed.
