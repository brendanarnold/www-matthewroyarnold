Ready to hang

Example from the Taste and Image Reaction series

Looking for an artwork to match your home? In the Taste Studies, individual discs are presented as ready-to-hang works which are uniquely matched to owner's tastes – the ones in the slideshow for example are inspired by the Cassini Mission to the Saturnian system, others have been inspired by pop culture. Typically these works are on a framed glass background with a disc composition overlaid resulting in an attractive minimalist piece. Specify a precise composition and palette to exactly suit your own requirements for as little as £40 (plus delivery) for the smaller pieces, to £75 (plus delivery) for the larger works – email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com for more details.

Pre-made works from the Taste Studies are available occasionally put up for auction with no-reserve. To find out when this happens, subscribe to the news page on Facebook.

Looking for a unique gift? The Image Reaction Studies are tailored for individuals based on their interests. For example, the `Early Crimson' works shown were commissioned by an individual who supplied a series of giclée printed images of aviation and academic environments upon which the discs were overlaid. Prices begin at £40 with framed ready to hang versions for an extra £30 – again email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com for more details.
