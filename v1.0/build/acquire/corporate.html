Installations

Looking for a dynamic work that changes with season? The Place Reaction Studies can be installed by the artist in a composition and range of colours exactly suitable for your own business environment. Alternate sets of discs can be supplied with easily swapped Velcro mounts to allow the apparition paintings to be changed throughout the year giving a dynamic, seasonal change of colours to the space.

Previous clients have included the University of West England, Frenchay and a Place Reaction Study installation was exhibited in the Royal West of England gallery in 2010.

Prices start at £2,000 for a 3×5 m installation – for more details email matthewroyarnold@gmail.com

Ready to hang

The Taste Studies make an ideal hanging for private offices. The works feature clean lines combined with traditional artistic practice in oil paints. Large pieces begin at £75 (plus delivery) and even even larger pieces are available on request.
